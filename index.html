Mysterious surface features hint at a tumultuous past. A chance to study Earth's evil twin and understand climate. A journey to the inferno, where extreme science meets adventure! Mars, the fourth rock from the sun, beckons with its mystique: Rust-colored deserts and towering volcanoes. An otherworldly atmosphere, 95% carbon dioxide. A day slightly longer than Earth's, but with chilly nights. The potential for life hidden beneath the surface. The dream of being the first human on another planet!
